## Why not Libron / Cartisse / NV Palatium?

Those three were requested but are **not openly/redistributably licensed**:
"Libron" and "Cartisse" circulate as personal-use display faces (and display
faces read poorly as long-form body text), and "Palatino" is a Linotype
trademark — "NV Palatium" is a renamed knockoff. The OFL serifs above cover the
same intent; **`palladio` (URW Palladio / P052) is the genuine open Palatino**
clone. If you have properly-licensed copies of the originals, drop the `.ttf`s
in this folder and add an entry to `reader/fonts.py`.
